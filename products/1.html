QCK Sneaker 1
The QCK 1 is a top-notch performance basketball shoe. Starting off with the cushion, the shoe features a full-length midsole unit along with an extra forefoot unit that sticks out from the bottom. The midsole of the shoe is the material designed to absorb the forces of running and jumping. As a general principle of physics, the stiffer the midsole material, the better able the player to explode from the floor; the softer this material, the better cushioned the player will be. Both the cushioning and the traction on these are good for outdoor courts. The lightweight midsole combined with the forefoot unit makes for decent impact protection on both blacktop and concrete.

QCK 1 is a great option if you are looking for a light & stylish shoe with no real weaknesses.
