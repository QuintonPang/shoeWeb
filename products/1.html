QCK Sneaker 1
Take those first steps on your running journey in the all new QCK 1. Made from at least 20% recycled content by weight, it has a supportive fit and stable ride, with a lightweight feel that easily transitions from your workout to hangout. Your trek begins. Lace up and hit the road.
About The Shoe
Supersoft foam through the midsole helps cushion your foot with every step. The increased height means a softer sensation as you run. The rubber outsole has the traction to help keep your feet gripped to the pavement. Skins at the toes and eyelets offer durability. A mesh material shows off the internal components at the midfoot and toe.

Mesh throughout the upper has a lightweight, breathable feel. The mesh was placed in key zones based on runner feedback, providing you with cooling where it counts.
