QCK Sneaker 3
In a classic black and white colourway, these sneakers are cut from smooth, crisp leather in the upper for a durable wear. The midsole represents the spine, graduated panels are the muscles, the lace loops are the shoe's ribs and the mesh on the upper is the skin. Freshen up your footwear with these men's QCK 3. Exclusive in the summer release, these sneaks land in a black colourway and are built with a mesh upper for breathability and have synthetic overlays to add structure. They feature a lace fastening with a padded ankle collar for locked in fit and heel pull tab for easy on-and-off. Underfoot is a midsole that delivers lightweight cushioned comfort and a rubber outsole for durability and essential grip. Finished with QCK branding to the heel.

With a hard-wearing rubber outsole for standout traction, these trainers are finished off with iconic white stripes embroidered to the sidewalls.
